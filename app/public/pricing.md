# Serpely Pricing

Last updated: 2026-05-14

## Starter — Free

- **Price**: $0/month
- **Keywords**: Up to 100 keywords
- **Websites**: 1 website
- **Rank Tracking**: Weekly
- **Site Audits**: Weekly
- **GEO Score**: Read-only (view, not edit)
- **API Access**: No
- **AI Citation Monitor**: No
- **White-Label Reports**: No
- **Support**: Email support

## Professional — $49/month

- **Price**: $49/month (billed monthly) | $39/month (billed annually — saves ~20%)
- **Keywords**: Up to 1,000 keywords
- **Websites**: Up to 5 websites
- **Rank Tracking**: Daily
- **Site Audits**: Daily
- **GEO Score**: Full dashboard
- **API Access**: Yes
- **AI Citation Monitor**: Yes — tracks ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Google AI Overviews
- **White-Label Reports**: No
- **Support**: Priority email support
- **Free Trial**: 14 days, no credit card required

## Business — $99/month

- **Price**: $99/month (billed monthly) | $79/month (billed annually — saves ~20%)
- **Keywords**: Unlimited
- **Websites**: Unlimited
- **Rank Tracking**: Real-time
- **Site Audits**: Continuous
- **GEO Score**: Full dashboard
- **API Access**: Full API access
- **AI Citation Monitor**: Yes
- **White-Label Reports**: Yes — branded dashboards and automated reports
- **Dedicated Account Manager**: Yes
- **Support**: 24/7 priority support
- **Free Trial**: 14 days, no credit card required

## Notes

- All prices in USD
- Annual billing saves ~20% on paid plans
- 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans
- Plans can be upgraded or downgraded at any time
- Changes take effect at the start of the next billing cycle
